About Me

Hello! My name is Barry Yu! I am a tech-savvy, multi-faceted marketer who combines the creative and strategic thinking with the yang of results-driven execution and analytics. Born in China and raised in NYC, I'm bilingual marketing professional with 5 years of combined experience in digital marketing, spanning across B2B & B2C technology (ad tech, Martech), digital publishing, and Big Four Agencies (media, PR, and advertising).
